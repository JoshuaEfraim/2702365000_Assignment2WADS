Our Final Project

Our group is developing a help desk ticketing system for employees at Siloam Hospital, designed to efficiently manage and track issues. The system will feature priority levels to categorize and address urgent requests promptly. Admins will have access to a user-friendly dashboard, providing them with an overview of open, in-progress, and resolved tickets. The goal is to create a centralized system where all issues are tracked in one accessible dropbox, ensuring that no request is overlooked and that responses are streamlined. This solution will enhance communication, simplify issue resolution, and improve overall operational efficiency within the hospital.
